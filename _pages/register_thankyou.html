Thank you for Registering

We've got your registration -- see you at DjangoCon!

Those traveling to the Bay Area should checkout out our conference page for information on flights, hotel reservations, and such.

Those traveling to Google Sao Paulo should checkout our conference page for information, which will be updated shortly.

Thanks to our generous sponsors we've been able to make this conference free to attendees. However, the conference won't be free for us. We'll be helping some speakers afford the expense of the conference, printing programs and handouts, and other conference-y things.

To help with these costs, and to help us put on future events of this nature, please consider making a donation to the Django Software Foundation (all donations should be made in US dollars):

$

For more information, see the DSF's donation FAQ, or the more general foundation FAQ.

There's something in it for you, too: we'll have little "thank you" gifts at the conference for those who give $25 or more.

Thanks, and we can't wait to see you at DjangoCon!
